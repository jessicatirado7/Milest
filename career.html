Employment Opportunities

Milestone Mental Health is a group private practice that offers specialized mental health care for children and families. We have a constantly growing waitlist of clients, so we are seeking to grow our group of providers to match the need in our community! Our clinic offers two fully equipped play therapy offices (with room to add one more), an observation/coaching therapy room with a one-way window including listening/sound equipment, and individual offices.

Positions Available:

Mental Health Professional or Practitioner

Position:

  • Mental Health Professional or Pre-Licensed Mental Health Practitioner to provide outpatient mental health services in Oakdale, MN as an Independent Contractor
  • LPCC, LMFT, LICSW, LADC, or LP preferred but on licensure-track is acceptable as well

Description:

The ideal candidate would be licensed for at least 1 year, have experience providing outpatient services, and motivated to build their own practice. Preferred areas of specialization: EMDR trained or certified, Brainspotting, RPT or supervised by an RPT-S, Somatic Experiencing, working with children and families, early childhood (DC 0-5).

The position involves providing outpatient therapy, conducting diagnostic intakes, creating comprehensive treatment plans, coordinating client care with other providers, maintaining all client documentation/treatment notes, and carrying a caseload of approximately 15+ weekly clients. As an independent contractor you can create your own schedule and have your own office.

We offer a highly competitive fee split that is based on monthly revenue. Our clinic offers a steady base of referral sources, EHR, insurance credentialing, contracting and billing, collecting client balances, advertising and marketing, MN Care Tax, a HIPAA compliant phone system and email address, and the flexibility to set your own schedule. We are a supportive group of providers that believe in supporting one another on our professional journey, peer consultation is always available. If you are not fully licensed yet, supervision options may be available as well.
