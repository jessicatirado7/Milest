Circle of Security Parenting

Our therapists are certified Circle of Security Parenting (COS-P) facilitators. COS-P program is an evidence-based intervention for caregivers of children ages 0-12. COS-P is based on decades of research about how to support and strengthen security in caregiver-child relationships. When using COS-P, our therapists work with parents or caregivers to help them to:

  • Understand their child’s emotional world by learning to read emotional needs and cues
  • Support their child’s ability to successfully manage emotions and turn to caregivers when in need
  • Enhance the development of their child’s self-esteem
  • Honor their innate wisdom and desire for their child to be secure

COS-P can be delivered within the context of individual or family psychotherapy. We also periodically offer COS-P groups for caregivers. There are no groups being offered at this time; however, if you are interested in this service, please inquire to be added to a contact list for our future group offerings.
